The Jurisdictional Clarity Project

The Jurisdictional Clarity project was my first software engineering project in Python: a webtool for on-demand, district-specific data estimates. The tool was meant to help district-level officials quickly contextualize geographic issues ranging from wildfire risk to projected sea level rise on a local level.
