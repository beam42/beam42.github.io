The Jurisdictional Clarity Project

The Jurisdictional Clarity project is my first software engineering project: a webtool for on-demand, district-specific data estimates, meant to contextualize geographic issues ranging from wildfire risk to projected sea level rise on a local level.

CAD & 3D Printing: "Twisty Puzzles"

In my free time, I design and 3D print Rubik's Cube-type "twisty puzzles." In 2016, I founded a forum and file-sharing repository for fellow puzzle-makers, which hosts many of my designs and others'. The Puzzle-Makers group currently hosts 1,300+ members and 300+ designs. See my designs here .
