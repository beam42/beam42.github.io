In 2021 I completed my minor in journalism and resolved to abandon the field altogether.
In 2020 I covered Black Lives Matter protests, the COVID-19 pandemic, and more for my hometown newspaper, the Press Banner. When a wildfire forced communitywide evacuations, I reached out to nearby daily the Santa Cruz Sentinel, which published my reporting on the disaster.
In 2019 I published op-eds in the Los Angeles Times, Sacramento Bee, and Daily Californian, in which I advocated for a bill that would become California Proposition 18 in 2020.
