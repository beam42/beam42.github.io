The Pyramick (2020)

The pyramick is an original seven-axis puzzle that I first designed and 3D printed at home during the 2020 pandemic. It can be looked at as a pyraminx mech mod, or a hybrid between Uwe Meffert's Pyraminx and the "Gimmick" puzzle. Currently something big in the works with this puzzle!

The Twisted Flame (2020)

The Twisted Flame is another puzzle that I first designed and 3D printed during the 2020 pandemic. It's another pyraminx modification-- in the same spirit as Eitan's twisted cube and twisted skewb, and QiYi's twisted skew, this is not quite a twisted-pyraminx-- more like a twisted windmill pyraminx. See more here.

The Moldovian Pyramid (2020)

In 1982, after years of labor, Soviet toymaker Aleksandr Ordynets abandoned an almost-working prototype of his masterwork: the Moldovian Pyramid. In 2020, a bored American teenager used modern technology to design and build this version in two days, without the same fatal flaw.

The Reuleauxminx (2017)

Couldn't figure out whether I wanted to make a dodecahedral or tetrahedral puzzle, so this seemed like the obvious choice! It's a Face-Turning Dodecahedron transformed into a tetrahedron. Reuleauxminxes have been made before, but this is the first 3D printed one that I'm aware of.
