The Moldovian Pyramid (2020)

In 1982, after years of labor, Soviet toymaker Aleksandr Ordynets abandoned an almost-working prototype of his masterwork: the Moldovian Pyramid. In 2020, a bored American teenager used modern technology to design and build this version in two days. It has the same flaw.
